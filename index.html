02 — The Rulebook

League By-Laws

The by-laws that govern roster construction, scoring, waivers and fair play. Reach out to the commissioner if you have any questions.

  1. Loser’s Jersey

    The last-place finisher for the season must wear the loser’s jersey to the following year’s draft. Last place is determined by the lowest ranked team as of Week 14.

  2. Mandatory Trade

    Each manager must make at least one trade during the season. If not, they are ineligible to win the championship.

  3. Draft Day Attendance

    If you do not attend draft day (in person or via Zoom), you cannot have the 1st pick. You move down one spot until the first attending manager is eligible.

  4. Roster Lock

    Teams that do not make the playoffs have their rosters locked — no waiver adds/drops or lineup changes.
