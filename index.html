League Rules

Below is a concise overview of our league’s rules. These govern roster construction, scoring, waivers and fair play. Reach out to the commissioner if you have any questions!

  1. Loser’s Jersey Rule – The last-place finisher must wear the loser’s jersey to the following year’s draft.
  2. Mandatory Trade Rule – Each manager must make at least one trade during the season. If not, they are ineligible to win the championship.
  3. Buy-In Requirement – Each manager must pay a $100 buy-in before the season begins.
  4. Draft Day Attendance Rule – If you do not attend draft day (in person or via Zoom), you cannot have the 1st pick. You move down one spot until the first attending manager is eligible.
