League Rules

Below is a concise overview of our league’s rules. These govern roster construction, scoring, waivers and fair play. Reach out to the commissioner if you have any questions!

  1. Rosters consist of 1 Quarterback, 2 Running Backs, 2 Wide Receivers, 1 Tight End, 1 Flex (RB/WR/TE), 1 Kicker and 1 Defense/Special Teams.
  2. The draft is a snake draft with randomly determined order. Draft order reverses each round.
  3. Scoring is half‑point PPR: 6 points per TD (rush/rec), 4 points per passing TD, 0.5 point per reception, 1 point per 10 rushing/receiving yards and 1 point per 25 passing yards.
  4. Waivers are processed weekly using FAAB (Free Agent Acquisition Budget). Each team begins the season with 100 FAAB dollars.
  5. Trades must be completed by Week 10 and will be reviewed by the commissioner for fairness.
  6. The regular season runs through Week 14. Playoffs begin Week 15 with the top four teams competing in a two‑round, single‑elimination format.
  7. Unsportsmanlike conduct, collusion or tanking will result in penalties up to removal from the league.
